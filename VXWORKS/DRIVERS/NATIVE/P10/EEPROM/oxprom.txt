OXFORD 16C954 UART EEPROM UTILITY
=================================

This is a utility for dumping, writing and verifying the
original content of the serial EEPROM of the P10/P11.

This utility scans the PCI bus and selects the P10/P11
for manipulating their EEPROM data. This utility is 
generally made for writing the P10/P11 EEPROM while 
manufacturing.
